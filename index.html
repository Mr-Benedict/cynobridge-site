Cyno Bridge
EVE Online companion

Your capsuleers, at a glance

An unofficial companion app for EVE Online — track skills, wallet, clones, assets, location and planetary industry across all your characters, in one place.

Read-only · no in-game actions · your data stays on your device

Skills & queue

Skill points, training progress, and the full skill queue with finish times.

Wallet

Balance, journal, transactions, and your open market orders.

Clones & implants

Jump clones, their locations, and the implants in each.

Assets

What you own and where it sits, with reference-price valuation.

Location & ship

Current system, station/structure, online status, and active ship.

Planetary industry

Your PI colonies and extractor/factory status at a glance.
