Safe by Design

Your data never leaves your device.

Cyno Bridge has no servers of its own. It talks only to CCP's official EVE services, from your phone, using a login you authorize.

● Read-only access
Scoped, read-only ESI permissions. The app can never spend ISK, move assets, or change a thing in-game.
● No backend, no tracking
No developer servers, no analytics, no ads. We never receive, store, or even see your data.
● Tokens stay local
Login tokens live in your device's hardware-backed secure storage. Uninstalling wipes everything.
Questions

The short answers.

Is this affiliated with CCP Games?
No. Cyno Bridge is an unofficial, fan-made tool. EVE Online and all related trademarks are the property of CCP hf.
Can it do anything to my account?
No. You grant read-only scopes through EVE's official Single Sign-On. The app can view your data but never modify, trade, or move anything.
Where does my data go?
Nowhere but your screen. Character data is fetched directly from CCP's servers to your device and held only in memory to display it. There is no developer backend.
How many characters can I add?
As many as you like. Add each alt with one EVE SSO login and switch between them in two taps.
